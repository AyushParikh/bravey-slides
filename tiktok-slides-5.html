Mistake 02 / WTP
Ask.
You've never actually tested what customers will pay.
→

Founders guess. The Van Westendorp method takes 20 minutes and reveals your real ceiling.

→

Ask: "At what price does this feel expensive but still worth it?" That's your anchor.

→

Most founders land 40–60% below their customers' actual willingness to pay.

Mistake 03 / Anchoring
3 tiers
One price = lost revenue. Tiers make the middle sell itself.
→

A single plan forces a yes/no. Three tiers create a choice — and choice increases conversion.

→

Make your high tier look almost reasonable next to the premium one. Anchoring is free money.

→

The middle tier should be your actual target. Build up and down from there.
